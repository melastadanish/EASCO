## SECTION 4: FAQ

**H2: Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q: What's the difference between Solutions pages and Product pages?**
A: Solutions pages show you which product combinations work for your specific facility type—which turnstiles, which biometric systems, which door openers fit your industry's unique requirements. Product pages show you the detailed specifications of each individual system. Start with your industry, then drill into the products recommended there.

**Q: My facility spans two industries. Which page should I start with?**
A: Start with the page matching your primary use case or highest-traffic zone. Then review the second industry's page for that specific zone. Contact us if your mix is unusual—we'll recommend a custom solution.

**Q: Do all EASCO products work in all industries?**
A: No. For example, outdoor-rated stainless steel turnstiles work for coastal facilities; aluminum turnstiles work better for indoor corporate offices. Each industry page lists only the products that fit that facility type's specific operating conditions.

**Q: How does access control differ between a hospital and a factory?**
A: Hospitals need privacy-conscious biometrics (palm vein instead of face recognition in sensitive areas) and round-the-clock visitor screening. Factories need sober-only policy enforcement, multi-shift attendance tracking, and spread-out site access. The hardware is the same; the configuration and control logic differ.

**Q: Can I see real examples of EASCO systems in use?**
A: Yes. Visit our [Case Studies](/case-studies/) section to see how EASCO systems are deployed across different venue types. For a specific reference in your region, contact us directly.

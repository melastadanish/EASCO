## S6 — FAQ

**H2: Frequently Asked Questions**

---

**Q: What throughput do EASCO turnstile gates handle?**
A: The cylindrical speed gate processes up to 45 persons per minute — enough to clear a 500-seat auditorium entrance in under 12 minutes at peak flow. Most corporate lobby deployments require 10–20 persons per minute. The 45 persons/min rating gives headroom for surge periods without queueing.

---

**Q: What happens if someone gets caught in the gate?**
A: The gate stops the instant it meets resistance. Crank-link transmission with magnetic-sensitive limit control is mechanical — not software-dependent. The ESYBZ and ESYAD models both include this as a standard feature. The gate does not trap; it stops and reverses.

---

**Q: What control systems do EASCO turnstile gates connect to?**
A: Standard interfaces are RS-485, TCP/IP, Wiegand 26/34, and Ethernet. The Android speed gate (ESYBZ LCD+Face) runs an onboard Android OS and does not require an external controller for face recognition access. For custom software integration, SDK and API documentation are available on request.

---

**Q: Can EASCO turnstile gates be installed outdoors?**
A: The face recognition terminal on the Android speed gate (ESYBZ LCD+Face) is IP65-rated for outdoor installation. For the gate body IP rating in your specific climate — particularly for coastal, industrial or high-humidity sites — send your site conditions and the technical team will confirm the right specification.

---

**Q: Do EASCO turnstile gates prevent tailgating?**
A: An IR sensor array detects a second person following the first through the open gate and re-closes before the second person passes. For transit-grade anti-tailgating requirements where throughput is also critical, the cylindrical gate configuration handles both — 45 persons/min with IR-based anti-tailgate active.

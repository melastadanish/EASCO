## Section 4 — What Are Face Recognition Accessories?

**H2: What Are Face Recognition Accessories?**

Face recognition accessories are the hardware components that transform a standalone biometric terminal into a fully integrated access control solution. EASCO's accessory range covers mechanical mounting (brackets, stands, enclosures), optical enhancement (infrared lighting, supplemental lenses), electrical integration (power supplies, relays, network modules), and cable assemblies for standard protocols (Wiegand, RS485, Ethernet). Unlike custom integration work that takes weeks and requires engineering, off-the-shelf accessories let facility managers deploy their biometric terminals in days. Integrators and facility managers use accessories to adapt EASCO terminals to existing door frames, retrofit outdoor installations with weather protection, or wire terminals into multi-building access control networks without separate servers or software licenses.

## Section 5 — Key Features of Face Recognition Accessories

**H2: Key Features of Face Recognition Accessories**

**Universal mounting compatibility — one bracket, any terminal, any wall.** EASCO mounting brackets adjust in two axes and rotate 360° without tools. They fit 7-inch, 8-inch, and 10-inch EASCO face terminals, plus competitive brands using standard VESA mounting. Install directly on drywall, concrete, or metal poles with stainless steel hardware included. No custom fabrication. No contractor markup.

**IR lighting that works in complete darkness without visible glare.** The dual-array 850nm infrared panel extends face recognition capability to outdoor installations, underground parking, and night-shift facilities. Six brightness levels let you dial in the right amount of illumination — bright enough for reliable recognition, dim enough that no visible light escapes. IP65-rated housing survives coastal salt spray and high-humidity environments.

**Pre-wired integration modules skip the electrician call-back.** Wiegand, RS485, and Ethernet connectors mount on DIN rail with surge protection built in. Connect your terminal to existing door locks, turnstiles, and access control panels without field-splicing cables or risk of ground loops. Power distribution, relay outputs, and network filtering come pre-configured in one module.

**Stainless steel and IP65 components handle outdoor and harsh environments.** Every fastener, bracket, and connector is either stainless steel (for coastal zones) or nylon (non-corrosive). Electrical enclosures are sealed to IP65, meaning they survive dust storms, salt fog, and washdown. No re-engineering needed for difficult sites.

## Section 8 — Frequently Asked Questions

**H2: Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q: Do accessories work with terminals from other manufacturers?**
A: EASCO mounting brackets use standard VESA mounting, so they fit most 7–10 inch terminals. Wiegand and RS485 integration modules follow industry-standard pin configurations. For specific compatibility, contact us via WhatsApp (+86 13711422283).

**Q: Will IR lighting interfere with regular surveillance cameras?**
A: No — 850nm infrared is invisible to human eyes and standard camera sensors. It only affects thermal imaging cameras. EASCO's IR kit is designed specifically for face recognition terminals and poses no interference risk.

**Q: Can I install the mounting bracket myself without a technician?**
A: Yes — EASCO mounting brackets require no tools and no drilling (adhesive mounts available). Electrical installation of power and network connections should follow local electrical codes; hire a licensed electrician if you're unsure.

**Q: What's the difference between EASCO brackets and generic mounting solutions?**
A: EASCO brackets are engineered for biometric terminals — they account for camera angle, IR lighting positioning, and cable routing. Generic brackets often force awkward angles or block air vents, reducing accuracy and causing overheating.

**Q: Can the IR lighting kit be retrofitted to my existing terminal?**
A: Yes — the dual-array panel connects via USB or network cable to any EASCO face recognition terminal. Installation takes minutes. Brightness is software-adjustable from the terminal's control panel.

**Q: What happens if I need custom integration for my specific access control system?**
A: EASCO offers custom integration services for Wiegand, Modbus, TCP/IP, and proprietary protocols. Lead time is typically 30–45 days. Contact our integration team via WhatsApp for a custom quote.

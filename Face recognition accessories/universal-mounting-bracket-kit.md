### SECTION 12 — FAQ

**H2: Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q: Does the universal bracket work with non-EASCO terminals?**
A: Yes. The bracket uses standard VESA mounting holes (75×75mm and 100×100mm), so it fits any 7–10 inch biometric terminal that supports VESA mounting — including Hikvision, ZKTeco, and other major brands. Confirm your terminal's VESA mounting pattern before ordering.

**Q: What's the difference between wall, pole, and corner mounting?**
A: Wall mounting uses drywall anchors or concrete screws on flat surfaces. Pole mounting uses a U-clamp assembly for round or square poles (1.5–2.0" diameter). Corner mounting uses a 90° bracket for tight spaces. All three variants are included in the standard kit.

**Q: Can I adjust the angle after installation?**
A: Yes. The 360° ball joint allows rotation and ±45° tilt adjustment at any time. Loosen the locking knob at the base, adjust the angle, then re-tighten. No tools required.

**Q: Will the bracket survive outdoor salt spray and humidity?**
A: Yes. The bracket frame is stainless steel 304L and all fasteners are A4-grade stainless steel, both rated for coastal environments. The elastomer damping pads are UV-stable neoprene. IP65 sealing on cable routing points prevents water ingress.

**Q: How long does installation take?**
A: Typical wall mount installation takes 5–15 minutes for a single terminal. Pole mounting takes 10–20 minutes. No electrician required — the brackets are mechanical only.

**Q: What's covered under warranty?**
A: Manufacturing defects (cracks, fastener corrosion, joint failure) are covered for 2 years. Wear from improper installation, impacts, or environmental exposure beyond IP65 rating is not covered. Remote diagnosis available via WhatsApp — most issues resolve without return.

## Writing Instructions

### Tier 1 Checks (Run after EVERY section before moving to next)
1. **Spec-backed:** Every claim traced to client-data-map.md or product specs
2. **Objection coverage:** Address at least 2 industry-specific objections from objection-map.md
3. **Differentiator presence:** Mention at least 2 EASCO differentiators (from differentiator-card.md)
4. **Keyword density:** Target keyword appears 3–5 times naturally
5. **CTA clarity:** Every major section has a clear next action

### Target Length
- Total: 1,800–2,400 words
- Section 1 (Hero): 150–200 words
- Section 2 (Challenges): 300–400 words
- Section 3 (Products): 400–500 words
- Section 4 (Diagram): 100–150 words (caption + context)
- Section 5 (Features): 300–400 words
- Section 6 (Case Study): 150–200 words
- Section 7 (Integration): 100–150 words
- Section 8 (FAQ): 400–500 words
- Section 9 (CTA): 100 words
- Section 10 (Related): 50 words

### Five-Pass Review (After full page written)

#### Pass A: Full-Page Quality Audit
Score each dimension 1–10:
1. **Spec accuracy** — all numbers traced to source
2. **Objection coverage** — addresses buyer fears specific to industry
3. **Buyer segment alignment** — speaks to Facility Manager or relevant role
4. **EASCO differentiation** — shows why EASCO vs competitors
5. **Keyword optimization** — natural SEO without stuffing
6. **Conversion clarity** — clear path to quote/contact

**Minimum: 8.0/10 overall**

#### Pass B: Surgical Rewrite
Fix ONLY sections that scored <8 in Pass A. Do not rewrite the entire page.

#### Pass N: NLP Semantic Coverage
Required industry-relevant terms must appear (varies by industry):
- "access control"
- "[industry name]" (e.g., hospital, school, office)
- At least 2 product category terms (turnstile, face recognition, etc.)
- Integration terms (if relevant to industry)

#### Pass D: E-E-A-T / GEO Check
- [ ] One AI-quotable paragraph (90–120 words, factual, citable)
- [ ] FAQ with 5+ Q&As specific to this industry
- [ ] Authority signals: ISO/CE mention, 20 years, 63 patents
- [ ] Freshness: "Updated [month year]"

#### Pass C: Conversion Review
- [ ] Test 1: CTA above fold ✓
- [ ] Test 2: WhatsApp contact visible ✓
- [ ] Test 3: At least 2 objections from objection-map.md covered ✓
- [ ] Test 4: At least 2 differentiators from differentiator-card.md present ✓
- [ ] Test 5: Clear next action at end of page ✓
- [ ] Test 6: AI-written score ≤ 15% (use detector tool)

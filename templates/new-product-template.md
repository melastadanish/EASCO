## Content Writing Guidelines

### Hero Stats Box Rules:
- Use **icon + number + label** format only
- Numbers must be specific (not "high," "fast," etc.)
- Label should be outcome-focused, not feature-focused
  - ❌ Wrong: "250kg capacity"
  - ✅ Right: "Max gate weight"

### Feature Block Rules:
- **H2 headline is the main benefit** — what the buyer gets, not what the product does
  - ❌ Wrong: "Hidden Motor Design"
  - ✅ Right: "The Motor Stays Hidden Below Floor"
- **Checkmark bullets must be outcomes or benefits**, not specs
  - ❌ Wrong: "Stainless steel construction"
  - ✅ Right: "Survives saltwater and corrosive environments"
- **3 checkmarks per block maximum** — no more

### Tab Content Rules:
- **Overview:** What is it? Who uses it? Why?
- **Details:** How does it work? Design philosophy. Real-world deployment scenarios.
- **Technical Specs:** Organized tables with category badges (pill buttons)

### Layout Alternation:
- Block 1: Image LEFT, Text RIGHT
- Block 2: Text LEFT, Image RIGHT
- Block 3: Image LEFT, Text RIGHT
- Pattern: Left-Right-Left-Right

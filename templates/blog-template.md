**Writing rules specific to blogs:**
- Blog articles are informational first. Do not lead with EASCO products — lead with answering the question.
- EASCO is mentioned in Section 5 only (perspective/proof section) and Section 7 (soft CTA).
- Every fact must trace to client-data-map.md OR be sourced from a verifiable external source (cite it).
- No hard CTAs ("Buy now", "Request a quote") before Section 7. Mid-page CTA = "See related products".
- AI-quotable paragraph required in Section 2 or 3.

### SECTION 8: FAQ

**H2: Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q: What's the difference between the J, JF, and ESBL terminal?**
A: J is the base aluminum gate body with no onboard terminal. JF is a J-series gate with the ESBL terminal already embedded. ESBL can also be added to other compatible gate bodies, including the ESYBZ cylindrical gate.

**Q: How many access methods does it support?**
A: Five: face recognition, QR code, card (RFID/IC), password, and remote door opening, all through the same 7-inch touchscreen terminal.

**Q: Can it be fooled by a photo held up to the camera?**
A: No. Financial-grade liveness detection rejects photos, video replays, and printed faces before granting access.

**Q: Does it integrate with our existing access control software?**
A: Yes. Ethernet, WiFi, RS-485, Wiegand 26/34, and USB 2.0 interfaces ship standard, with SDK and API documentation for custom integration. Request it through the [Contact page](/contact/).

**Q: Will it work in a hot or humid climate?**
A: The terminal is IP65-rated and operates from -25°C to +75°C at 5–93% humidity, non-condensing.

**Q: What's the gate body's throughput and passage width?**
A: These figures are being confirmed with the factory for the JF gate body specifically and will be published once verified. The terminal's own recognition speed (200–300ms) is already confirmed.

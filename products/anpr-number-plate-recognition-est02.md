## SECTION 8: HOW IT WORKS

**Eyebrow (red, uppercase):** HOW IT WORKS

**H2:** 4 Steps. Vehicle Entry to Access Decision.

Your ANPR system processes every vehicle in milliseconds:

**Step 1 — Vehicle Detection**  
As a vehicle approaches the monitored lane, motion sensors (or simply camera frame-change detection) trigger the 5MP camera. The camera captures a high-resolution image of the vehicle's front and rear license plate from the mounting angle and distance.

**Step 2 — Plate Recognition**  
The character recognition engine scans the plate image and extracts the license number, country code, and state (if applicable). Night vision and low-light algorithms activate if needed. The system flags partial or obscured plates and attempts alternative angle analysis. Recognition completes in milliseconds.

**Step 3 — System Lookup & Authorization**  
The plate number is transmitted via RS-485 to your parking management or toll system. Your backend database queries: Is this plate registered? Is the permit valid? Is the account paid? Authorization decision is made and returned to the ANPR unit in real-time.

**Step 4 — Output & Logging**  
The system outputs voice ("Access granted" or "Not registered"), displays the plate and status on the 18.5-inch screen, and optionally controls a barrier gate relay. The event is logged with timestamp and photograph. Revenue is calculated for toll scenarios, and the driver is informed of the amount due.

**Q1: What countries' license plates can EASCO ANPR read?**

**Q2: How accurate is plate recognition in heavy rain or fog?**  
A2: The 5MP camera and night-vision algorithms handle rain and low visibility. Heavy fog (< 100 metres visibility) may require closer mounting or supplemental lighting. We recommend discussing your specific climate conditions with EASCO before installation.

**Q3: Can I integrate ANPR with my existing parking management software?**  
A3: Very likely. ANPR connects via RS-485 transparent transmission — your software receives plate number and timestamp. If your parking system accepts these inputs (most modern systems do), integration is straightforward. Confirm with us before purchase.

**Q4: What's the maximum vehicle speed the system can recognize?**  
A4: The system handles vehicles moving at up to 40 km/h (25 mph) without loss of accuracy. Higher speeds may reduce recognition reliability. For toll highways (70+ km/h), discuss higher-speed camera options with EASCO.

**Q5: Is the voice output customizable?**  
A5: Yes. The system includes pre-recorded human voice announcements. TTS (text-to-speech) expansion is available for custom messages specific to your facility rules.

## 8. How It Works

**Eyebrow:** HOW IT WORKS

**H2:** 4 Components. One Gate That Opens Every Time.

**Subtitle:** Your ESYBZ or ESYAD is a simple system: motor, control logic, safety sensors, and power. Each part does one job perfectly.

**Step 1 — The Motor**
A DC brushless servo motor drives the crank-link transmission. When triggered (by card, face, or button), power flows through the 24V DC control module. The servo ramps up smoothly—no jerking start—and stops calmly at full open. The entire cycle takes 0.3 seconds. No mechanical shock. No wear.

**Step 2 — The Controller**
The 24V DC control board listens to your access system via RS-485, Ethernet, WiFi, or MQTT. When an authorized person triggers access (face match, card read, or manual button), the controller energizes the motor. It can handle wired RS-232, legacy Wiegand inputs, or modern MQTT brokers—whatever your system speaks.

**Step 3 — The Safety System**
Magnetic-sensitive limit control sets mechanical boundaries: when the gate reaches full-open or full-close, a magnet trips a reed switch, cutting power. The crank-link transmission is mechanical—if the gate hits an obstacle mid-cycle, the linkage stalls instantly and the controller trips. No crushing. No damage. Collision avoidance is built into the physics of the machine.

**Step 4 — Power Backup and Manual Release**
In a power loss, the gate stays where it stopped. During emergency evacuation or alarm, the manual release lever lets anyone open the gate by hand—no power needed. Add an external battery backup (UPS) for automatic return-to-open on blackout.

## 9. FAQ

**Eyebrow:** COMMON QUESTIONS

**H2:** What People Ask Before Buying a Cylindrical Speed Gate

**Q1: How fast is the 0.3-second opening really?**
**A1:** 0.3 seconds is the time from your access trigger (card swipe, face match, or button press) to fully-open gate. Your foot crosses the threshold about 0.5 seconds after triggering—well within normal pedestrian stride. This speed is measurable and verified in factory testing. No marketing hype.

**Q2: Can I use the ESYAD with my existing access control system?**
**A2:** Yes. The ESYAD speaks RS-485, Ethernet, TCP/IP, WiFi, MQTT, Wiegand, and WebSocket. If your current system outputs any of these protocols, the gate integrates with a single cable. No gateway device. No proprietary middleware required.

**Q3: What happens if someone blocks the gate while it's opening?**
**A3:** The crank-link transmission is mechanical. When the gate meets resistance, the linkage stalls immediately and the controller de-energizes the motor. The gate stops dead. No crushing force. The magnetic limit control won't re-trigger until the obstruction is cleared and the gate fully resets.

**Q4: How wide can I customize the passage?**
**A4:** 600–1500mm—that's roughly 24–60 inches, covering standard doorway width at most facilities. If your opening is outside this range, contact us for custom engineering. Customization adds 5–10 business days to lead time.

**Q5: Does the display version (ESYAD LCD+Face) work in low light?**
**A5:** The face recognition uses a 2MP infrared camera plus a 2MP RGB wide-dynamic camera. It works in lighting from full sun to indoor office light, down to twilight outdoor conditions. Pitch-black requires infrared illumination only—the system handles it, but we recommend additional ambient lighting for user comfort.

**Q6: What's the lead time?**
**A6:** Stock models ship 5–10 business days factory-direct from Shenzhen. Customized width or configuration adds 5–7 days. Large-volume orders (50+ units) ship on a weekly cadence. Contact us via WhatsApp (+86 13711422283) for exact lead time on your order.

### SECTION 6: How It Works

**H2: One Gate, Configured Entirely From Its Own Screen**

1. A facility manager configures access methods (face, card, QR, password) directly on the gate's onboard 7-inch touchscreen, without a separate control panel.
2. A user presents credentials at the gate: a face scan, a card tap, a QR code, or a password entry.
3. The onboard Android terminal verifies the credential in 200–300ms for face recognition.
4. On a valid match, the gate mechanism releases, no signal has to travel to an external controller first.

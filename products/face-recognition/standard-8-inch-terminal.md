### SECTION 6: How It Works

**H2: Face Capture to Access Signal in Under a Second**

1. A person approaches the terminal within 0.5–3 metres of the camera.
2. The terminal captures the face and checks for liveness, rejecting a photo or screen replay.
3. It matches the live face against the enrolled database in 200–300ms.
4. On a valid match, an access signal releases the connected door or turnstile via Wiegand or RS-485.

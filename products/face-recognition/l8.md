### SECTION 6 — How It Works

**H2: Face First, Fingerprint as the Built-In Fallback**

1. A person approaches the terminal, and the camera attempts face recognition against the enrolled database.
2. If a face covering, low light, or camera angle prevents a confident match, the person places a finger on the fingerprint module instead.
3. The fingerprint check verifies against the same enrolled record in 0.8 seconds.
4. On a valid match by either method, the access signal releases the door or turnstile via Wiegand or RS-485.

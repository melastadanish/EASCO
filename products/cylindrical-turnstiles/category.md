### SECTION 8: FAQ

**H2: Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q: What's the difference between ESYBZ and ESYAD?**
A: Both are cylindrical speed gate bodies with the same 45 persons/min throughput and 0.3-second opening speed. Each is available as a base gate or with the LCD+Face Android terminal. Confirm which body suits your access control setup with EASCO directly.

**Q: How fast does the gate process people?**
A: 45 people per minute, opening in 0.3 seconds, fast enough to clear a 500-seat auditorium in under 12 minutes.

**Q: Is the gate body rated for outdoor use?**
A: The gate body's IP rating is not yet confirmed by the factory, so it isn't claimed here. The LCD+Face terminal's own housing is IP65-rated. Confirm outdoor suitability for the gate body with EASCO before specifying an uncovered installation.

**Q: Can someone get injured if the gate closes on them?**
A: Crank-link transmission with magnetic-sensitive limit control stops and reverses the gate on contact, a mechanical safeguard, not a software timeout.

**Q: Does it integrate with our existing access control system?**
A: Yes. The gate supports Ethernet, RS-232, RS-485, TCP/IP, WiFi, WebSocket, MQTT, and Wiegand 26/34, covering most existing access control platforms without custom middleware.

**Q: Can we get one-way and two-way gates in the same order?**
A: Yes. Direction is configured per gate at order stage, so a single project can mix one-way and two-way lanes as needed.

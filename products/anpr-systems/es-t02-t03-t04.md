### SECTION 6: How It Works

**H2: From Plate to Barrier Signal in Real Time**

1. A vehicle approaches, and the 5MP camera captures the plate regardless of angle, glare, or partial dirt coverage.
2. The system recognizes the plate and displays it on the 18.5-inch screen for the driver to confirm.
3. A real human voice or TTS announcement confirms the result audibly.
4. The recognized plate transmits via RS-485 to the connected parking management platform, which authorizes the barrier or gate to open.

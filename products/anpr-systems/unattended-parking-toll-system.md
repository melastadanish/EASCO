### SECTION 6 — How It Works

**H2: From Plate Recognition to Barrier Release, No Staff Required**

1. A vehicle approaches, and the system's LPR camera captures the plate.
2. The system logs the entry against the plate and, on exit, calculates any parking fee due.
3. The driver pays via POS, cash, coin, or a prepaid parking card at the unit.
4. On successful payment, the barrier releases automatically; if a problem occurs, the driver presses the one-touch call button to reach a remote administrator.

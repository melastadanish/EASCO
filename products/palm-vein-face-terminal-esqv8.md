## 8. How It Works

**Eyebrow:** HOW IT WORKS

**H2:** 2 Biometrics. One Terminal. Complete Identity Verification.

**Subtitle:** Your ESQV8 verifies identity twice—palm and face—before your door unlocks.

**Step 1 — Palm Vein Enrollment**
During initial setup, you place your hand on the scanner (140–400mm distance). The near-infrared sensors capture your unique vein pattern in under 0.5 seconds. The system stores this pattern locally on the 32GB encrypted storage. One scan, biometric captured.

**Step 2 — Face Recognition Scan**
The IR and RGB cameras simultaneously capture your facial features from 0.5 to 3 metres away. The dual-camera system works in sunlight, indoor light, and twilight. Liveness detection confirms your face is real (not a photograph). Both biometrics captured in a single scan.

**Step 3 — Dual-Match Decision**
The recognition engine compares both live scans against the stored templates in < 0.5 seconds. If both palm vein and face match, the display shows "PASSED" in green. If either fails, the display shows "DENIED" in red and alerts management.

**Step 4 — Relay Activation & Logging**
If both biometrics match, the ESQV8 sends a relay signal to your door strike or barrier gate, unlocking entry for 1–2 seconds (configurable). The access event logs to your access control system via Ethernet/RS-485/Wiegand. Your visit is timestamped and searchable.

## 9. FAQ

**Eyebrow:** COMMON QUESTIONS

**H2:** What People Ask Before Installing an ESQV8

**Q1: What's the difference between palm vein and palm print?**
**A1:** Palm print (fingerprint of the palm) reads surface features—can be dirty, scarred, or worn smooth. Palm vein reads the subsurface vascular pattern beneath the skin—immutable and unique. Spoofing palm vein is impossible because veins are under the skin. Print spoofing is theoretically possible with a high-quality fingerprint copy. ESQV8 uses vein, not print.

**Q2: Does face recognition work in winter or poor light?**
**A2:** Yes. The ESQV8 combines 2MP RGB (wide-dynamic for varying light) and 2MP infrared cameras. It works in daylight, office lighting, and twilight. In pitch darkness, the IR camera alone can trigger recognition—no visible light needed. For maximum user comfort, we recommend 100+ lux ambient light (a typical hallway).

**Q3: Can I integrate ESQV8 with my existing access control software?**
**A3:** Very likely. ESQV8 supports Ethernet, WiFi, RS-485, Wiegand, and USB. If your access control software accepts Wiegand input (standard on most systems), ESQV8 integrates with a single cable. No custom development. Check your system's network output options and contact us via WhatsApp to confirm compatibility.

**Q4: How many faces can ESQV8 store?**
**A4:** 50,000 faces and 10,000 palm vein patterns locally. When enrollment nears capacity, you can archive old records to cloud storage or export to USB. For sites with fewer than 1,000 regular users, capacity is unlimited for practical purposes.

### SECTION 6 — How It Works

**H2: A Simple Arm Mechanism With Full Safety Coverage**

1. A person presents credentials via card, remote release, or an optional face recognition add-on.
2. On a valid match, the single arm retracts smoothly, with the ambient light switching from blue to green.
3. Infrared sensors monitor for tailgating throughout the passage, and anti-pinch sensors protect against contact injury during arm movement.
4. On a power outage, the gate fails open rather than trapping people at the entrance.

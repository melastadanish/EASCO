### SECTION 6: How It Works

**H2: Palm Vein as the Primary Check, Face Recognition as a Verified Second**

1. A person holds a palm near the sensor, and near-infrared imaging captures the subsurface vein pattern.
2. The terminal matches the pattern in under 0.5 seconds against the enrolled database.
3. Face recognition can run as an alternative or supplementary check, verifying at 99.98% accuracy.
4. On a valid match by either method, the access signal releases the connected door or turnstile.

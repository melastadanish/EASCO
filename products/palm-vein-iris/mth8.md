### SECTION 6: How It Works

**H2: Iris First, With Face Recognition as a Second Verified Path**

2. The iris scanner captures the pattern and matches it against the enrolled database in under 2 seconds, regardless of ambient lighting.
3. Face recognition can run as an alternative or secondary check, verifying at 99.98% accuracy in 200–300ms.
4. On a valid match by either method, the access signal releases the connected door or turnstile.

### SECTION 6: How It Works

**H2: A Palm Held Near the Sensor, No Contact Needed**

1. A person holds a palm 140–400mm from the sensor, without touching any surface.
2. Near-infrared light captures the subsurface vein pattern, distinct from any surface feature like a fingerprint or skin tone.
3. The terminal matches the pattern against the enrolled database in under 0.5 seconds.
4. On a valid match, the access signal releases the door or turnstile via Wiegand or RS-485.

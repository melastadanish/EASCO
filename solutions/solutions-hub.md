### SECTION 2: Industries Served

**H2: Six Sectors, Six Different Priorities**

#### [Commercial Buildings](/solutions/commercial-buildings/)

Staff flow and visitor management matter more than raw throughput. Flap barriers and face recognition cover most office lobbies without over-specifying for traffic that never arrives.

#### [Smart Campus / Schools](/solutions/smart-campus/)

Student attendance and visitor screening run on the same platform. Card and face recognition combine for daily attendance, with visitor check-in handled at the gate.

#### [Transportation Hubs](/solutions/transportation/)

Airports, metro stations, and bus terminals need throughput above all else. Cylindrical and speed gate turnstiles clear crowds fast, paired with ANPR for vehicle access points.

#### [Hospitals](/solutions/hospitals/)

Patient privacy and staff access don't share the same rules. Palm vein recognition avoids storing facial data where that's a regulatory concern, while face recognition handles general staff flow.

#### [Factories / Warehouses](/solutions/factories/)

Attendance accuracy and safety compliance drive the spec. Alcohol detection combined with face recognition enforces a sober-only policy at the door, not on the honor system.

#### [Government Buildings](/solutions/government/)

Security tier outranks convenience. Iris recognition's 1-in-10,000,000 misrecognition rate suits the highest-security zones, backed by turnstile access for general staff areas.

## SECTION 2: Challenges in Factories and Warehouses

**H2: What Industrial Access Control Actually Has to Solve**

**Sober-only policy enforcement.** A written safety policy doesn't stop an impaired worker from clocking in unless something checks for it at the door.

**Attendance across a large, spread-out floor.** A single fixed terminal at the front gate doesn't cover a warehouse aisle or a remote loading dock.

**Buddy punching on shared cards.** A card-only system can't stop one worker from clocking in for a colleague who isn't actually on site.

**Harsh environmental conditions.** Dust, humidity, and temperature swings common on a factory floor shorten the life of hardware not built for it.

## SECTION 2: Challenges in Schools and Campuses

**H2: What Campus Access Control Actually Has to Solve**

**Attendance accuracy without a manual roll call.** A teacher taking attendance by hand doesn't scale across a campus with hundreds of students moving between buildings.

**Buddy check-in on shared student cards.** A card-only system can't stop one student from checking in a friend who isn't actually present.

**Visitor and parent screening.** A campus needs to know who's on site without turning every parent pickup into a security interrogation.

**Budget constraints across multiple entrances.** A school with 5–10 entrances can't specify a premium speed gate at every single one.

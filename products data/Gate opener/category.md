## Section 4 — What Is an Automatic Gate Opener?

**H2: What Is an Automatic Gate Opener?**

An automatic gate opener is an electric motor assembly that moves a gate or door on a control signal — a remote press, an access card read, a vehicle ground loop, or a timed schedule. The motor connects to the gate via a floor spring pivot, a gear-and-rack rail, or a direct arm mount, depending on the gate's movement type. When the signal arrives, the opener moves the gate to the open position, holds it for the dwell time, and returns it to closed.

EASCO manufactures five gate opener models. The floor spring controller (BB-DS-01) is designed for single-swing glass doors — 48W, 1.2m maximum width, recessed installation below the door pivot. The gear-and-slide-rail opener (BB-WSCLCT-01) drives heavy sliding gates up to 1,000kg using a rack-and-pinion mechanism with 3× overload capacity and -40°C cold-weather resilience. The heavy-duty servo opener (BB-DD-01) handles the largest commercial and industrial applications — 6-metre span, 1,100kg gate weight, 1.2KW continuous power, 5 million cycle service life. The horizontally mounted and side-mounted folding arm models cover specialist configurations for specific installation geometries. All three fully specified models operate below 36VDC at the gate mechanism — safe for wet outdoor environments without electrical hazard at the gate surface.

## Section 5 — Key Features

**H2: Key Features of EASCO Gate Openers**

**24–36VDC motor voltage eliminates electric shock risk at the gate.** The input draws from 220VAC mains, but the motor voltage at the gate mechanism is 24–36VDC — below the threshold that can injure a person on contact. At a wet outdoor car park entry or a poolside gate, operators and users are never exposed to mains voltage from the moving hardware.

**3× overload capacity on the gear-and-slide-rail model.** The BB-WSCLCT-01 runs at 550W rated but can sustain 1,500W for short durations. When a heavy sliding gate hits a partial obstruction — a vehicle blocking the path, ice build-up on the rail — the motor pushes through rather than stalling. The floating limit operation ensures straight-line travel regardless of minor rail flex.

**5 million cycle service life on the heavy-duty model.** The BB-DD-01 uses a permanent magnet synchronous motor controlled by Hall sensor feedback. At 200 gate operations per day, 5 million cycles equals approximately 68 years of continuous service. The absolute encoder replaces traditional limit switches — no manual recalibration needed as gate mechanics shift over time.

**WeChat Mini Program remote control and diagnostics.** All three specified models support remote open/close control, fault reporting, usage cycle tracking, and time-window scheduling through a WeChat Mini Program. Gate status, error codes, and operational data are available remotely without on-site visits.

## Section 8 — Frequently Asked Questions

**H2: Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q: What gate weight and width can EASCO gate openers handle?**
A: The range spans from single-swing glass doors (BB-DS-01, up to 1.2m width) through heavy sliding gates (BB-WSCLCT-01, up to 1,000kg) to large industrial gates (BB-DD-01, up to 6 metres wide and 1,100kg). Contact EASCO with your gate dimensions and weight for model confirmation.

**Q: Why does the motor voltage matter for safety?**
A: All three fully specified models operate the gate mechanism at 24–36VDC — below the voltage level that poses an electric shock hazard to humans on contact. The 220VAC mains input is converted at the controller enclosure, not at the gate. This is particularly important for wet outdoor installations, pool areas, and car parks.

**Q: What is the operating temperature range?**
A: The BB-WSCLCT-01 gear-and-slide-rail model operates from -40°C to +70°C — the widest range in the product line. The floor spring (BB-DS-01) and heavy-duty (BB-DD-01) models operate from -25°C to +70°C. All models are suitable for outdoor installation.

**Q: How long does the heavy-duty motor last?**
A: The BB-DD-01 permanent magnet synchronous motor is rated for 5 million operating cycles. At 200 gate operations per day, that equates to approximately 68 years of continuous commercial use.

**Q: What remote control and automation options are available?**
A: All three specified models support IR remote control, hard-wired relay signals from access control systems, and WeChat Mini Program remote management including open/close control, schedule setting, fault reporting, and cycle count tracking. RS485 serial communication is available on all models.

**Q: Can the gate opener integrate with a face recognition terminal?**
A: Yes — all models accept hard-wired relay signals from access control systems. EASCO face recognition terminals connect directly to the gate opener's signal input via the relay output. Contact EASCO for wiring schematics and integration guidance.

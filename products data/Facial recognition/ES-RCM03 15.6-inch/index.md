## 8. How It Works

**Eyebrow:** HOW IT WORKS

**H2:** Face Presented. Recognized. Access Granted. 4 Steps.

**Step 1 — Presence Detection**
IR camera detects the user at 0.5–3 m. Liveness detection confirms a live person — not a photo or screen.

**Step 2 — Recognition**
RGB camera captures the face. Onboard processor matches against up to 50,000 templates at 99.98% accuracy in 200–300 ms.

**Step 3 — Access Signal**
Relay switch, Wiegand 26/34, or RS485 command opens the gate or lock. The recognition result and attendance record display on the 15.6-inch screen.

**Step 4 — Stats Update & Media Resume**
The event is logged and the display returns to slideshow or advertising content until the next recognition event.

## 9. FAQ

**Eyebrow:** COMMON QUESTIONS

**H2:** What Buyers Ask Before Ordering the ES-RCM03

**Q1:** What is the difference between the ES-RCM03 and the ESJ 8-inch terminal?
**A1:** The ES-RCM03 has a 15.6-inch 1920×1080 display and supports advertising video and image slideshows between access events. The ESJ has an 8-inch display and carries an IP65 rating for outdoor use. The ES-RCM03 is rated indoor only. Both share the same 99.98% accuracy and 50,000-face onboard capacity.

**Q2:** Can it display advertising content?
**A2:** Yes — image slideshows and advertising video play on the 15.6-inch panel between recognition events. The screen switches to the recognition result display during each access event, then returns to media content.

**Q3:** Which orientations are supported?
**A3:** Both horizontal (landscape) and vertical (portrait). Orientation is specified at order time.

**Q4:** What interfaces does it support?
**A4:** Wiegand 26/34, RS485, TCP/IP Ethernet, WiFi 2.4 GHz, USB 2.0, and relay switch output. SDK and API documentation available.

**Q5:** Is it suitable for outdoor installation?
**A5:** No — the ES-RCM03 is rated for indoor use only. For outdoor or industrial environments, the ESJ (IP65) is the correct model.

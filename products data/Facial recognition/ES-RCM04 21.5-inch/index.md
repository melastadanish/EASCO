## 8. How It Works

**Eyebrow:** HOW IT WORKS

**H2:** Face Presented. Recognized. Displayed. 4 Steps.

**Step 1 — Presence Detection**
IR camera detects the user at 0.5–3 m. Liveness detection confirms a live person.

**Step 2 — Recognition**
RGB camera captures the face. Onboard processor matches against up to 50,000 templates in 200–300 ms at 99.98% accuracy.

**Step 3 — Access Signal**
Relay switch, Wiegand 26/34, or RS485 opens the connected gate or lock. The result — name, photo, time — displays large on the 21.5-inch panel.

**Step 4 — Stats Update & Media Resume**
Attendance statistics update in real time. The display returns to slideshow or advertising content until the next event.

## 9. FAQ

**Eyebrow:** COMMON QUESTIONS

**H2:** What Buyers Ask Before Ordering the ES-RCM04

**Q1:** What is the difference between the ES-RCM04 and the ES-RCM03?
**A1:** The ES-RCM04 has a 21.5-inch display versus the ES-RCM03's 15.6-inch. Both share the same 1920×1080 resolution, 99.98% accuracy, and 50,000-face capacity. Choose the ES-RCM04 where the terminal is placed at a distance from users or where the attendance display needs to be read from across a room.

**Q2:** Can it display advertising content simultaneously with attendance functions?
**A2:** Yes — advertising video and slideshows run between recognition events. During each access event, the screen switches to the recognition result and attendance data, then returns to media content.

**Q3:** Is real-time attendance tracking available?
**A3:** Yes — the display shows signed-in, signed-out, and pending counts in real time directly on the 21.5-inch panel, without needing a separate monitor or PC.

**Q4:** What interfaces are supported?
**A4:** Wiegand 26/34, RS485, TCP/IP Ethernet, WiFi 2.4 GHz, USB 2.0, and relay switch output.

**Q5:** Is it suitable for outdoor use?
**A5:** Indoor use only. Use the ESJ (IP65) for outdoor environments.

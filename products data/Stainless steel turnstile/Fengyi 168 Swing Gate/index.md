## 8. How It Works

**Eyebrow:** HOW IT WORKS

The selected credential module processes the authorization. External button or remote control operates the gate without a module fitted.

## 9. FAQ

**Eyebrow:** COMMON QUESTIONS

**H2:** What Buyers Ask Before Ordering the Fengyi 168 Swing Gate

**Q1:** How does the gas-strut lid design help in practice?
**A1:** Loosen two screws and the lid lifts to a stable open position automatically — hands stay free to work inside the cabinet. No risk of the lid closing unexpectedly mid-maintenance. It significantly reduces the time and skill required for inspections.

**Q2:** How does the Fengyi 168 differ from the Fengchang 108?
**A2:** The 168 has a larger-diameter cylinder (more substantial feel), gas-strut lid maintenance, 10-million-cycle motor, and 40 persons/minute throughput. The Fengchang 108 uses a drawer-rail maintenance design, 15-million-cycle motor, and 50 persons/minute throughput. Choose the 168 where premium feel and easy maintenance outweigh throughput priority.

**Q3:** Are infrared sensors included?
**A3:** No — infrared sensors are an optional add-on (1 pair). The optional sensor is strongly recommended where children or high-density foot traffic is present.

**Q4:** Is it suitable for outdoor installation?
**A4:** Yes — rated for indoor and outdoor use. Install on a 100–200 mm concrete platform outdoors and add a weather canopy.

**Q5:** What is the maximum passage width?
**A5:** 1,500 mm — wider than the 108 model's 1,200 mm maximum. Suitable for passages that need to accommodate trolleys or wheelchair access.

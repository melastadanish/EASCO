## 8. How It Works

**Eyebrow:** HOW IT WORKS

**H2:** Credential In. Gate Swings. 4 Steps.

**Step 1 — Identity Verification**
The selected credential module processes the authorization. External button or remote control operates the gate without a module fitted.

**Step 2 — Controller Signal**
The ARM control core sends an open signal via RS485, RS232, or IO relay. Wiegand 26/34 output connects to third-party platforms.

**Step 3 — Gate Movement**
The brushless motor swings the gate panel open in under 0.5 seconds. Throughput reaches 50 persons per minute.

**Step 4 — Safety & Reset**
On power cut, the gate opens and the ARM core retains position — no recalibration on restart.

## 9. FAQ

**Eyebrow:** COMMON QUESTIONS

**H2:** What Buyers Ask Before Ordering the Fengchang 108 Swing Gate

**Q1:** What makes the Fengchang 108 different from the 168 model?
**A1:** The 108 uses a drawer-rail maintenance design and is rated for both indoor and outdoor use with a 15-million-cycle motor at 50 persons/minute. The 168 has a larger φ168 mm cylinder, gas-strut assisted lid, 10-million-cycle motor, and 40 persons/minute throughput. Choose the 108 for higher throughput; choose the 168 where easier maintenance matters more.

**Q2:** Does it come with infrared anti-crush sensors?
**A2:** No — infrared sensors are an optional add-on (1 pair). The base unit does not include automatic obstacle detection. Add the sensor option at order time for sites with children or high-volume traffic.

**Q3:** Is it suitable for outdoor installation?
**A3:** Yes — rated for indoor and outdoor use. For outdoor deployment, install on a 100–200 mm concrete platform to prevent ground moisture ingress and add a weather canopy.

**Q4:** What credential modules are available?
**A4:** Card reader, QR code scanner, ID card / passport reader, and face recognition panel — all available as separate add-ons.

**Q5:** What is the motor lifespan?
**A5:** 15 million continuous open/close cycles on the brushless motor. Periodic lubrication every six months; full inspection annually.

## 7. How It Works

**Eyebrow:** HOW IT WORKS

**H2:** Credential In. Gate Open. 4 Steps.

**Subtitle:** Every Interstellar gate follows the same control flow — from identity check to passage — in under a second.

**Step 1 — Identity Verification**
The face recognition panel detects the user at 0.5–3 m and completes recognition in 200–300 ms at 99.98% accuracy. The comparison runs on the onboard processor — no cloud round-trip needed for access decisions. Card or QR code readers process the credential in parallel if that method is selected.

**Step 2 — Controller Signal**
The ARM industrial control core receives the verification result and sends an open signal via RS485, RS232, or IO relay. The decision is immediate. Wiegand 26/34 output is available for third-party access control platform integration.

**Step 3 — Gate Movement**
The direct-drive servo motor opens the gate panel in under 0.3 seconds. The passage stays open for the configured dwell time before the gate resets. Throughput reaches 50 persons per minute at continuous flow.

**Step 4 — Safety & Reset**
If resistance is detected mid-swing, reverse braking and three-stage torque compensation engage before the arm makes contact. Infrared sensors re-close the gate if a second person follows through. On power cut, the gate opens automatically and the ARM core retains position — no recalibration on restart.

**Eyebrow:** COMMON QUESTIONS

**H2:** What Buyers Ask Before Ordering the Interstellar Speed Gate

**Q1:** How many people per minute can the Interstellar gate process?
**A1:** Over 50 persons per minute in continuous flow. The direct-drive servo motor opens in under 0.3 seconds, which eliminates the gap between authorization and passage. Passage width is configurable from 550 mm to 1,200 mm.

**Q2:** Will it connect to our existing access control system?
**A2:** Yes. Gate-open signals are accepted via RS485, RS232, or IO relay — standard interfaces on most access control platforms. HTTP/HTTPS, WebSocket, and MQTT are supported for software integration. Hardware SDK and device API are available for custom builds.

**Q3:** What access methods does it support?
**A3:** Face recognition (99.98% accuracy, 100,000-face capacity), IC/ID card, QR code, and optional ID card / passport module — all on a single integrated panel. Multi-modal combinations are supported simultaneously.

**A4:** Reverse braking engages automatically when resistance is detected, with three-stage torque compensation. The mechanism is hardware-level — not software-dependent — so it reacts before the arm makes contact.

**A5:** The Interstellar series is rated for indoor use. For outdoor installation, the Xingyao Waterproof series (IP65-rated) is the correct choice. Operating temperature range for both is -20°C to +75°C.

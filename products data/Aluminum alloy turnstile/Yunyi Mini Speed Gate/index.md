**Eyebrow:** SAFETY & PROTECTION

**H2:** Anti-Crush Built In. Six Infrared Pairs as Standard.

**Body:**
Six infrared sensor pairs monitor the full passage. When resistance is detected mid-swing, reverse braking and three-stage torque compensation engage before contact — this is hardware-level protection, not software-only. On power cut or fire alarm signal, the gate opens and locks open; the ARM controller retains position so no recalibration is needed on restart.

**Safety features:**
- 6 infrared sensor pairs — standard
- Reverse braking + 3-stage torque compensation on resistance

## 8. How It Works

**Eyebrow:** HOW IT WORKS

**H2:** Credential In. Gate Open. 4 Steps.

**Subtitle:** Every Yunyi mini gate follows the same control flow — from credential check to passage — in under a second.

**Step 1 — Identity Verification**
The selected credential module — card, QR, or face recognition panel — processes the authorization signal. External button or remote control operates the gate without a module.

**Step 2 — Controller Signal**
The ARM control core receives the result and sends an open signal via RS485, RS232, or IO relay. Wiegand 26/34 output is available for third-party access control integration.

**Step 3 — Gate Movement**
The brushless motor opens the gate in under 0.5 seconds. Six infrared sensor pairs monitor the passage throughout. Throughput reaches 50 persons per minute.

**Step 4 — Safety & Reset**
Resistance triggers reverse braking and three-stage torque compensation. On power cut, the gate opens automatically and the ARM core retains position — no recalibration on restart.

## 9. FAQ

**Eyebrow:** COMMON QUESTIONS

**H2:** What Buyers Ask Before Ordering the Yunyi Mini Speed Gate

**Q1:** How much smaller is the Yunyi compared to a standard speed gate?
**A1:** Approximately one-third smaller in floor footprint. The passage width tops out at 900 mm (versus 1,200 mm on the Starship 4 and Interstellar series). It suits narrow retrofits and spaces with fixed wall-to-wall constraints.

**Q2:** Does the compact size reduce throughput?
**A2:** No. The Yunyi processes 50 persons per minute — the same as the full-size gates in the aluminum alloy range. The smaller body only affects the footprint, not the motor or throughput spec.

**Q3:** Does it have infrared anti-crush protection?
**A3:** Yes — 6 infrared pairs ship as standard on both single and dual-lane models. Reverse braking and three-stage torque compensation engage on resistance detection before contact is made.

**Q4:** What credential modules can be added?
**A4:** Card reader, QR code scanner, ID card / passport reader, and face recognition panel (7-inch Android) are all available as add-ons.

**Q5:** Is it suitable for outdoor use?
**A5:** Indoor rated. For outdoor sites, the Xingyao Waterproof series (IP65) is the correct choice.

## 7. How It Works

**Eyebrow:** HOW IT WORKS

**H2:** Credential In. Gate Open. 4 Steps.

**Subtitle:** Every Xingyao Waterproof gate follows the same control flow — from identity check to passage — in under a second.

**Step 1 — Identity Verification**
The face recognition panel detects the user at 0.5–3 m and completes recognition in 200–300 ms at 99.98% accuracy. The onboard processor handles the comparison locally — no cloud round-trip needed. Card or QR readers run in parallel if that credential is selected.

**Step 2 — Controller Signal**
The ARM industrial control core receives the verification result and sends an open signal via RS485, RS232, or IO relay. Wiegand 26/34 output is available for third-party platform integration.

**Step 3 — Gate Movement**
The brushless motor opens the gate in under 0.5 seconds. The passage stays open for the configured dwell time before resetting. Throughput reaches 50 persons per minute at continuous flow.

**Step 4 — Safety & Reset**
Resistance triggers reverse braking and three-stage torque compensation before contact. Infrared sensors re-close the gate on tailgate detection. On power cut, the gate opens automatically and the ARM core retains position — no recalibration on restart.

## 7. How It Works

**Eyebrow:** HOW IT WORKS

**H2:** Credential In. Gate Open. 4 Steps.

**Subtitle:** Every Xingyun gate follows the same control flow — from credential check to passage — in under a second.

**Step 1 — Identity Verification**
The selected credential module — card, QR code, or face recognition panel — processes the authorization signal. The comparison runs on the ARM control core locally.

**Step 2 — Controller Signal**
The ARM industrial control core sends an open signal via RS485, RS232, or IO relay. Wiegand 26/34 output is available for third-party access control integration.

**Step 3 — Gate Movement**
The brushless motor opens the gate in under 0.5 seconds. The passage holds open for the configured dwell time. Throughput reaches 50 persons per minute per lane.

**Step 4 — Safety & Reset**
Resistance triggers reverse braking and three-stage torque compensation. On power cut, the gate opens automatically and the ARM core retains position — no recalibration on restart.

## 8. FAQ

**Eyebrow:** COMMON QUESTIONS

**H2:** What Buyers Ask Before Ordering the Xingyun Water Drop Speed Gate

**Q1:** What makes the Xingyun different from a standard dual-lane speed gate installation?
**A1:** The shared centre-column design replaces the middle pillar of a conventional two-lane setup with one shared column. That reduces floor space used and the number of pillars to install. The water-drop profile keeps the visual feel open while maintaining the same 50 persons/minute throughput per lane.

**Q2:** Does it come with infrared anti-tailgate sensors?
**A2:** Infrared sensors are an optional add-on (1 pair). The base unit ships without them. Buyers who need automatic tailgate detection should add the sensor option at order time.

**Q3:** What credential modules are available?
**A3:** Card reader, QR code scanner, ID card / passport reader, and face recognition panel (7-inch Android) are all available as add-ons. The base unit accepts a remote control or external button signal as standard.

**Q4:** Is it suitable for outdoor use?
**A4:** No — the Xingyun is rated for indoor use. For outdoor installation, the Xingyao Waterproof series (IP65-rated) is the correct product.

**Q5:** What is the motor lifespan?
**A5:** 15 million continuous open/close cycles. The brushless motor requires no brush replacement and minimal scheduled maintenance beyond periodic lubrication of mechanical parts.

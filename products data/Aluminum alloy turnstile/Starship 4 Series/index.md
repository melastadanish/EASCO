## 7. How It Works

**Eyebrow:** HOW IT WORKS

**H2:** Credential In. Gate Open. 4 Steps.

**Subtitle:** Every Starship 4 gate follows the same control flow — from identity check to passage — in under a second.

**Step 1 — Identity Verification**
The face panel detects users at 0.5–3 m, completing recognition in 200–300 ms at 99.98% accuracy. The onboard processor runs the comparison locally. Card and QR code readers process credentials in parallel.

**Step 2 — Controller Signal**
The ARM industrial control core receives the result and sends an open signal via RS485, RS232, or IO relay. Wiegand 26/34 output connects to third-party platforms without custom integration.

**Step 3 — Gate Movement**
The brushless motor opens the gate in under 0.5 seconds. The passage holds open for the configured dwell time before resetting. Throughput reaches 50 persons per minute in sustained flow.

**Step 4 — Safety & Reset**
Resistance triggers reverse braking and three-stage torque compensation. Infrared sensors detect tailgating and trigger re-close. On power cut, the gate opens and the ARM core retains position — no recalibration on restart.

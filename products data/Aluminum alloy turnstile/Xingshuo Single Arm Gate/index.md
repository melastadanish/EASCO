## 7. How It Works

**Eyebrow:** HOW IT WORKS

**H2:** Credential In. Arm Lifts. 4 Steps.

**Subtitle:** Every Xingshuo gate follows the same control flow — from credential check to passage.

**Step 1 — Identity Verification**
The selected credential module — card, QR, or face recognition — processes the authorization signal. Without a module fitted, an external button or remote control triggers the open signal.

**Step 2 — Controller Signal**
The ARM control core receives the result and sends an open signal via RS485, RS232, or IO relay. Wiegand 26/34 output connects to third-party access control software.

**Step 3 — Gate Movement**
The servo motor lifts the arm in under 1.5 seconds. The arm holds for the configured dwell time, then resets. Throughput reaches 30 persons per minute.

**Step 4 — Safety & Reset**
On power cut or fire alarm, the arm opens and locks open. The ARM core retains the arm's position — no recalibration is needed when power returns.

## 8. FAQ

**Eyebrow:** COMMON QUESTIONS

**H2:** What Buyers Ask Before Ordering the Xingshuo Single Arm Gate

**Q1:** What passage width does the Xingshuo support?
**A1:** 550 mm to 1,700 mm, configurable at order time. The 1,700 mm maximum accommodates bicycles, trolleys, and small non-motorised vehicles — wider than any flap barrier in the EASCO range.

**Q2:** Does it come with infrared anti-crush sensors?
**A2:** No — infrared sensors are an optional add-on (1 pair). The base unit does not include automatic obstacle detection. The sensor add-on is strongly recommended for sites with children or high-volume traffic.

**Q3:** What credential modules can be added?
**A3:** Card reader, QR code scanner, ID card / passport reader, and face recognition panel are all available as separate add-ons. The base unit operates via external button or remote control signal only.

**Q4:** Is it suitable for outdoor installation?
**A4:** Rated for indoor use. For outdoor deployment, raise the installation on a 100–200 mm concrete platform and add a weather canopy as described in the installation manual.

**Q5:** What is the motor lifespan?
**A5:** 15 million continuous open/close cycles on the DC servo + planetary reducer motor. Minimal maintenance — periodic lubrication of mechanical components every six months.

## 7. How It Works

**Eyebrow:** HOW IT WORKS

**H2:** Scan. Verify. Sync. Built for High-Volume Field Operations.

**Step 1 — Scan or Verify**
Press the dedicated scan button to activate the 2D engine for barcode or QR code reading. Tap the NFC zone for card reading. The rear 16 MP camera handles document or label capture.

**Step 2 — Process Locally**
The Qualcomm octa-core processor handles verification and data entry in real time. Face recognition applications can run via the front camera when installed.

**Step 3 — Transmit**
4G or dual-band WiFi syncs data to the management platform in real time. GPS/GNSS logs the location of each scan event for field workforce management applications.

**Step 4 — Continue**
The detachable battery keeps the device running through a full shift. Carry a spare battery and swap without powering down — zero downtime in the field.

## 8. FAQ

**Eyebrow:** COMMON QUESTIONS

**H2:** What Buyers Ask Before Ordering the ESR366-S

**Q1:** What scenarios is the ESR366-S designed for?
**A1:** Site attendance management, factory and warehouse inventory, logistics operations, and retail store management. Any workflow requiring mobile barcode scanning, NFC card reading, and 4G-connected data sync in the field.

**Q2:** Can the battery be swapped without shutting the device down?
**A2:** Yes — the 4,600 mAh battery is detachable. Carry a spare and hot-swap in the field. The optional charging cradle charges both the device and a spare battery simultaneously.

**Q3:** What scanning capabilities does it have?
**A3:** 2D barcode engine (accuracy ≥3 mil), NFC (13.56 MHz, ISO 14443A/B, ISO 15693, Mifare, NFC tags), and a 16 MP autofocus rear camera for document and label scanning. PSAM slots are available as an optional configuration.

**Q4:** What 4G bands are covered?
**A4:** Two regional variants: CN version (China/India bands) and EAU version (Europe/Middle East/Africa/Korea/Thailand/Australia bands). Specify your destination region at order time.

**Q5:** What is the operating temperature range?
**A5:** -10°C to 50°C operating, -40°C to 70°C storage. Humidity up to 95% RH non-condensing.

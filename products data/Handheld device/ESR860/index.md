**H2:** Receipts and Labels On-the-Spot. No External Printer Needed.

**Body:**
The built-in thermal printer prints at 70 mm/s on 30 mm diameter × 57 mm wide paper rolls, supporting both labelled paper and receipt paper simultaneously. **Printer specs:**

- Print speed: 70 mm/s (high pressure, high speed)
- Paper: 30 mm (diameter) × 57 mm (width) — supports labelled paper and receipts simultaneously
- Sensors: black label sensing, paper-out detection, overheat and over-voltage protection

## 9. FAQ

**Eyebrow:** COMMON QUESTIONS

**H2:** What Buyers Ask Before Ordering the ESR860

**Q1:** What is the print speed and paper size?
**A1:** 70 mm/s thermal printing on 30 mm diameter × 57 mm wide paper rolls. The printer handles labelled paper and receipt paper simultaneously — no setting changes required between print types.

**Q2:** How long does the battery last?
**A2:** Over 350 hours standby on a 5,600 mAh detachable battery. Charge time is 3–4 hours. Carry a spare battery for extended field deployments — swap without powering down.

**Q3:** How does the ESR860 differ from the ESR366-S?
**A3:** The ESR860 adds an integrated thermal printer and a larger 5,600 mAh battery. The ESR366-S is lighter (under 300 g vs under 500 g for the ESR860) and suited to scanning-only workflows that don't require on-the-spot printing.

**Q4:** What is UHF RFID and when is it needed?
**A4:** UHF RFID reads passive tags at up to 2 m range without direct contact — used in logistics warehousing for pallet or carton-level tracking, medical traceability, and library management. It is an optional add-on on the ESR860.

**Q5:** What 4G bands are supported?
**A5:** Two regional variants — CN (China/India) and EAU (Europe/Middle East/Africa/Korea/Thailand/Australia). Specify the destination region at order time.

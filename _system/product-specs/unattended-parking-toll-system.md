## Key Selling Points

- Fully unattended — no staffing required at the barrier
- Android system with secondary development support
- Integrated mold — aviation-grade aluminum, 100-year rust resistance
- IP65 protection level
- High-definition camera + microphone + one-touch call button — driver can reach administrator instantly
- Supports multiple payment methods: POS, change machine, coin machine, card swallowing machine

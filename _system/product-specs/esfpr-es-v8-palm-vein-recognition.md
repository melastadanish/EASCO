## Key Selling Points

- Unique appearance design
- Palm vein recognition — ultra-high security, latest recognition function
- No face image stored or processed — privacy-compliant for hospitals, financial institutions, government
- Subsurface infrared vein imaging cannot be replicated by photograph or surface copy
- Trimodal: palm vein + face + card in one unit

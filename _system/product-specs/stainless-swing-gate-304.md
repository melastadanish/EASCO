## Key Selling Points

- Waterproof design — applicable to various places including outdoor
- Compact body — cylindrical column design
- Application of hydraulic lifting rods
- Easy installation and maintenance
- Infrared sensors distributed up and down (full height coverage)
- Available in 108mm and 168mm diameter cylindrical body
- Three new stainless models added to range: round-corner swing gate, round-corner speed gate, right-angle swing gate

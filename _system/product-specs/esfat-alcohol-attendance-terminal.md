## Key Selling Points

- 1:1 binding of face recognition and alcohol detection — prevents proxy testing (ensures the tester is the actual person)
- Blocks alcohol-impaired individuals from operating vehicles at the source
- Prohibits intoxicated employees from entering hazardous work areas (chemical plants, mining sites)
- Single device replaces two separate checks

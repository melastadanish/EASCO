## Key Selling Points

- Waterproof, dustproof, shockproof (IP67) — genuine field-grade hardware
- Full-day battery — 5000mAh detachable battery
- 16MP autofocus rear camera for face recognition
- 4G SIM card slot — works without WiFi on remote sites
- Rich optional add-ons: NFC, QR code reader, receipt printer, UHF reader
- Android 13 — current OS, supported SDK/API development

> Note from client briefing: Do not limit to face recognition. Frame around hardware utility — barcode scanning, retail, warehouse, goods management, site access.

## Key Selling Points

- Designed to upgrade any turnstile gate to intelligent biometric entry
- Integrated design — simple and elegant, zero screws visible on body
- 5 door-opening methods in one unit: face, QR code, card, password, remote door opening
- Android OS with SDK/API support for custom integrations
- Convenient interfacing — connects to existing gate infrastructure

## Key Selling Points

- Industry pioneer: "Visual Interaction" — 18.5-inch screen shows recognition result to driver, doubles security confidence
- 18.5-inch HD screen unlocks advertising revenue during idle periods
- 5MP camera with universal adjustment — no fixed mounting constraints
- Integrated mold, aluminum — 100-year rust resistance claim
- Unencrypted hardware — compatible with any parking management platform
- Supports recognition of license plates from all countries
- Real human voice pronunciation + TTS expandable

## Key Selling Points

- Double-movement design with registered design patent
- Integrated mold made — aviation-grade aluminum alloy throughout
- Compact body — reduces space pressure vs. standard gates
- Configurable: card reader, face recognition, or remote controller
- ESYBZ LCD+Face variant: onboard Android face terminal — no external controller

## Safety Features

- Anti-pinch: stops and reverses on resistance contact
- Crank-link transmission with magnetic-sensitive limit control (mechanical — not software-dependent)
- Anti-tailing: IR sensor array

Stainless Steel Right-Angle Swing Gate Product Specifications
一、Product Overview
The Stainless Steel Right-Angle Swing Gate is an intelligent access control device for pedestrian passages. The entire unit is precision-crafted from SUS304 stainless steel, with corners processed using a right-angle cutting technique. It features bold, angular lines, a clean and sleek design, and a sturdy, durable structure. Equipped with a 24V DC brushless motor and paired with a next-generation industrial-grade ARM control system, it operates smoothly, quietly, safely, and reliably. The device supports bidirectional access control and features core functions such as automatic reset, anti-reverse protection, multi-level anti-collision buffering, and automatic gate opening in the event of a power outage. It also includes built-in infrared anti-pinch protection, fire alarm integration, and remote backend management capabilities. Combining rugged durability, intelligent control, and a bold, sleek aesthetic, it is the ideal choice for high-traffic, all-weather access scenarios.

二、Use Cases
1. Access Control: Orderly one-way or two-way passage for pedestrians, large suitcases, small hand carts, and lightweight non-motorized vehicles;
2. Integrated Peripherals: Facial recognition terminals, IC/ID card readers, QR code scanners, ID card readers, wireless remote controls, audible and visual alarms, and fire alarm integration modules; includes a free PC management platform and WeChat mini-program;
3.Suitable Locations: Industrial parks, construction sites, enterprises and institutions, residential communities, tourist attractions, school campuses, sports venues, and other locations requiring access control.

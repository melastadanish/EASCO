**Full-Height Turnstile Product Specifications**
**I. Product Introduction**
The full-height turnstile is a fully enclosed 360° cross-turnstile pedestrian access control device. The entire unit is constructed using a one-piece, thickened stainless steel molding process and is equipped with a servo drive control system. It is available in two models: single-lane and dual-lane; It features a full-height, enclosed anti-climbing structure, bidirectional access control, and anti-tailgating and anti-climbing designs. It supports integration with various access control devices, including facial recognition, card swiping, fingerprint scanning, and QR codes. Built-in features include infrared anti-pinch protection, automatic gate opening upon power failure, fire alarm integration, and remote backend management. The system operates smoothly and quietly, making it suitable for enclosed entrances and exits requiring strict personnel control, such as factory premises, construction sites, prisons, and industrial parks.

**II. Application Scenarios**
1. Access Control Scenarios: Enclosed personnel access channels; single-person, one-way or two-way access control;
2. Gate Channel Specifications
 Single-Lane Model: A single, independent rotating gate allowing one person to pass through at a time;
 Dual-Lane Model: Two gates operating in parallel to separate traffic flows and improve throughput;
3. Integrated Peripheral Devices: Facial recognition terminals, IC/ID card readers, fingerprint scanners, QR code scanners, visitor management systems, LED access display screens, audible and visual alarms, and fire alarm modules;
4. Applicable Locations: Real-name registration access points at construction sites, industrial park entrances and exits, prisons and detention centers, military bases, logistics parks, enclosed school campuses, gated residential communities, and subway maintenance access corridors.

**III. Product Features**
1. Full-height enclosure | Prevents climbing over and tailgating for strict access control
2. Servo drive | Smooth, quiet operation with cushioned start and stop
3. Infrared sensor grid | Full-range detection to prevent pinching and collisions
4. Multi-recognition compatibility | Supports facial recognition, card swiping, and QR codes
5. Fire safety integration | Automatically unlocks for evacuation in the event of a power outage
6. Cloud-based backend | Real-time access log tracking

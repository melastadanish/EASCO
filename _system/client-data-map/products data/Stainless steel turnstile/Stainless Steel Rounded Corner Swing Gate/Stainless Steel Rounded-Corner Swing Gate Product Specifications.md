**Stainless Steel Rounded-Corner Swing Gate Product Specifications**
**I. Product Introduction**
The stainless steel rounded-corner swing gate is a high-end smart access control device for pedestrian passageways. The entire unit is precision-crafted from SUS304 stainless steel, with rounded corners that feature smooth, flowing lines, combining collision protection with visual appeal. Equipped with a brushless DC gear motor, it operates smoothly, quietly, safely, and reliably. The device supports bidirectional access control. The swing gates are available with either acrylic or tempered glass panels, and the passage width can be flexibly customized. It features built-in infrared anti-pinch protection, fire alarm integration, power failure protection, and remote backend management. Combining aesthetic design, safety protection, and intelligent control, it is the ideal choice for modern entrance and exit management.

**II. Application Scenarios**
1. Access Control: Orderly one-way or two-way passage for pedestrians, large suitcases, small carts, and lightweight non-motorized vehicles;
2. Model Types
 Single-mechanism: A single, independent passage lane, suitable for entrances and exits with small to medium traffic volumes;
 Dual-mechanism: Parallel dual lanes for traffic diversion, designed to handle high-traffic scenarios during morning and evening rush hours;
3. Integrated Peripherals: Facial recognition terminals, IC/ID card readers, QR code scanners, ID card readers, palm print recognition, wireless remote controls, audible and visual alarms, and fire alarm integration modules; includes a complimentary PC management platform and WeChat mini-program;
Suitable Locations: Mid- to high-end access control venues such as commercial office buildings, industrial parks, residential communities, tourist attractions, school campuses, subway stations, airport waiting areas, enterprises and public institutions, and conference centers.

Stainless Steel Speed Gate Product Specifications
一、Product Overview
Stainless Steel Speed Gates are high-end intelligent access control devices for pedestrian passageways. The entire unit is precision-crafted from SUS304 stainless steel and features a reinforced round-tube frame structure. With a minimalist, modern design and a sense of openness, they ensure secure access control while maintaining visual transparency. Equipped with an integrated DC servo and planetary gear drive system, they offer rapid response and smooth operation, enabling quick gate opening for passage. The system supports bidirectional access control. The swing doors are available in either acrylic or tempered glass, and the passage width can be flexibly customized. It features built-in infrared anti-pinch protection, fire alarm integration, power failure protection, and remote backend management, combining efficient access, safety protection, and aesthetic design into one integrated solution.
二、Use Cases
1. Access Control: Orderly one-way or two-way passage for pedestrians, large suitcases, small carts, and lightweight non-motorized vehicles;
2. Integrated Peripherals: Facial recognition terminals, IC/ID card readers, QR code scanners, ID card readers, palm print recognition, wireless remote controls, audible and visual alarms, and fire alarm integration modules; includes a free PC management platform and WeChat mini-program;
3. Suitable Locations: Office buildings, government agencies, technology parks, CBD business districts, convention and exhibition centers, high-end residential communities, banks and financial institutions, airport VIP lanes, hotel lobbies, and other high-end venues requiring access control for personnel.

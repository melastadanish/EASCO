## 1.2 Installation warning

Before installation, please confirm whether the product meets the application requirements
(see "3.1 Pre-installation check"). If not, do not install this product.

power supply disconnected.

When installing, please be careful to contact this product, avoid collision, compression,
falling or contact with any liquid. This product should be placed away from high temperature,
open fire. The above situation may cause motor damage. If this problem occurs, stop installation
immediately and contact the company's technical department.

Do not modify any part without authorization. Otherwise, such operation may cause
equipment failure. The company shall not be liable for any equipment damage caused by
modifying the product.

If the door body installed with this product has a small door, the automatic door system
should be installed with a control device, so that the automatic door cannot operate when the small
door is open, to ensure safety.

Waste such as packaging materials of this product shall be disposed of in accordance with
local laws.

This product is not suitable for children, disabled persons or people who do not have

for their safety.

Avoid children touching the motor and control equipment. Keep the remote control out of

### 5.3 The remote control matching door opener and clearing data

5.3.1 Remote Pairing: Press the "Match" button for three seconds until the digital
display shows "- -0". Then press any key on the first remote control to display "- -1",
followed by another key on the second remote to show "- -2", and continue this
sequence until the display shows "- -N". Finally, press the "OK" button to complete
the pairing process for N remote controls.
5.3.2 Remote control code clearing: Press the "code matching" button for three
seconds. The digital tube displays — N (N represents the number of remote control
paired successfully). Then press and hold the "on" button for three seconds. The
digital tube displays — 0, indicating successful code clearing. Press the confirmation
key to return to normal standby state.

### 5.4 Adjustment of motor direction

When the direction of the switch door is reversed during debugging, you need to
enter menu P-31, set the motor direction, and then debug.

### 5.5 Debugging and zeroing

After the door motor is assembled and passes QC inspection, it can be powered
on for testing. Each power-up cycle displays "000" on the digital display. Upon

### 5.6
### age

After debugging is completed, the device can be placed into aging mode for
testing. Press the "Off" button for 3 seconds to activate aging mode. During this
process, the first digital display tube will move vertically and horizontally toward the
center. The control board will detect navigation signal operation. No sensor signal
reciprocation detection occurs during aging testing. The system will exit aging mode
after pressing the "Off" button again for 3 seconds.

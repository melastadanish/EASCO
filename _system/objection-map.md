# Objection Map — EASCO

> Every objection a B2B buyer has before ordering from a Chinese access control manufacturer.
> Every objection must be answered somewhere on every product page.
> Answer format: outcome first, spec or fact as proof.

---

## How to Use

1. Before writing any page — read this list
2. During Pass C Test 3 — check the page covers every relevant objection
3. Answers go into existing sections — do not create new sections for single objections
4. Mark which objections apply to which page types (all pages / product only / pillar only)

## Universal Objections — Must Be Answered on Every Page

These apply to every page on the site. A buyer from any country landing on any page carries these.

---

### O1 — "Is this a real factory or a trading company?"

**The fear:** Hundreds of Chinese "manufacturers" on Alibaba are trading companies reselling other factories' products with no quality control and no accountability. Buyers have been burned.

**The answer:**
> EASCO manufactures in-house at its Shenzhen facility — same address, same team, same production line for 20 years. Factory audit available on request.

**Where to answer:** Homepage hero, About Us, Why Choose Us, factory page
**Differentiator:** D1, D2

---

### O2 — "Will the quality be consistent order to order?"

**The fear:** First sample is good. Production batch is different. No recourse once goods are shipped.

**The answer:**
> ISO 9001:2015 quality management system governs every production run (certified by Wozhong Certification, valid to 14 Jun 2026 — see `_system/client-data-map.md` Certifications table). Each unit is tested before packing. Inspection reports provided on request.

**Where to answer:** Why Choose Us, Factory page, product pages
**Differentiator:** D1, D7

**Where to answer:** Certifications page, product pages (near spec table), footer
**Differentiator:** D1

### O5 — "Will it integrate with my existing system?"

**The fear:** Buys hardware. Hardware arrives. Cannot connect to existing access control software, fire alarm panel, or building management system.

### O15 — "Is palm vein more private than face recognition?"

**Where to answer:** Palm vein page, biometrics pillar, FAQ

**The answer:**

**Where to answer:** S2 pillar comparison table, individual product pages
**Differentiator:** D4 ✅

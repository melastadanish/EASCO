## Competitor Profiles

---

### ZKTeco

**URL:** zkteco.com
**Position:** Global access control and time attendance market leader. Largest volume player.
**Products:** Fingerprint + face readers, time attendance terminals, turnstile gates, parking systems.
**Weakness vs EASCO:**
- No palm vein. No iris recognition.
- OEM/ODM not a core offering — positioned as brand-first.
- No airport/kiosk solutions.
- Android integrated gate: not offered.
- Liveness detection: consumer-grade (anti-photo only), not financial-grade multi-modal.

**How to position against ZKTeco:**
> "ZKTeco leads on volume. EASCO leads on biometric range. For environments where face recognition alone isn't enough — high-security zones, contactless requirements, or privacy-sensitive spaces — EASCO manufactures palm vein and iris systems that ZKTeco doesn't offer."

---

### Hikvision Access Control

**URL:** hikvision.com/en/products/Access-Control-Products/
**Position:** Camera-first brand that extended into access control. Massive global distribution.
**Products:** Face recognition terminals, card readers, flap barriers. Core business is CCTV.
**Weakness vs EASCO:**
- Access control is a secondary product line — not a specialist manufacturer.
- No OEM/ODM for access hardware.
- No palm vein, no iris.
- No airport / transit solutions.
- Geopolitical procurement risk in some markets (US, Australia, UK government restrictions on Hikvision products).

**How to position against Hikvision:**
> "Hikvision's access control range is an extension of their camera business. EASCO is a specialist access control manufacturer — face, palm vein and iris under one roof, with OEM/ODM capability and airport-grade custom solutions that Hikvision doesn't build."

---

### Suprema

**URL:** supremainc.com
**Position:** Premium South Korean biometrics brand. Known for fingerprint + face, high accuracy, enterprise positioning.
**Products:** Face recognition, fingerprint readers, access control software.
**Weakness vs EASCO:**
- Korean manufacturing = higher price point.
- No palm vein, no iris commercial products.
- No OEM/ODM — brand-only positioning.
- No turnstile gates or vehicle access in core range.
- No airport / transit solutions.
- Less competitive on price for volume orders.

**How to position against Suprema:**
> "Suprema is positioned for high-end enterprise. EASCO is factory direct from Shenzhen — same biometric accuracy, broader product range including palm vein and iris, with OEM capability and pricing that makes large deployments viable."

---

### TurbooTurnstile

**URL:** turbooturnstile.com
**Position:** Turnstile-specialist Chinese manufacturer. Focused on gates, limited biometric depth.
**Products:** Speed gates, flap barriers, tripod turnstiles.
**Weakness vs EASCO:**
- Turnstile-only — no biometric terminals, no LPR, no attendance, no airport.
- No multi-modal biometrics in-house.
- No alcohol detection attendance.
- No OEM/ODM capability confirmed.

**How to position against TurbooTurnstile:**
> "TurbooTurnstile makes gates. EASCO makes the complete access control system — the gate, the biometric reader on it, the LPR camera at the vehicle entrance, and the attendance terminal in the lobby. From one factory."

### Generic Alibaba Suppliers

**Position:** Trading companies (not manufacturers) reselling factory products with no accountability.
**Weakness vs EASCO:**
- Cannot audit the factory because there is no factory.
- Quality is inconsistent order-to-order.
- No after-sales support.
- Certifications are often self-certified or purchased.
- No OEM/ODM — they cannot customise what they don't manufacture.

**How to position against Alibaba traders:**
> "Most access control listings on Alibaba are trading companies — they buy from the same factories you could source directly, with a margin added and accountability removed. EASCO is the manufacturer. Same address, same team, same production line for 20 years. Factory audit available."
